About Shreeja AI

We are building trust and governance foundations for agentic systems so organizations can scale AI operations responsibly.

Company overview

Based in Delhi, India, we focus on practical governance tooling that helps teams align innovation with control, transparency, and accountable outcomes.

Founded 2025 Delhi, India AI Governance

Mission

Enable organizations to deploy AI agents with confidence through clear runtime governance and operational guardrails. We believe trust should be engineered into systems, not bolted on after the fact.

Vision

Advance a future where trustworthy autonomous systems are standard in enterprise workflows. As AI agents take on more complex tasks, governance must evolve alongside their capabilities.

Values

Trust by design, operational clarity, and responsible human oversight across every stage of adoption. We build for the teams who need to ship fast without compromising on accountability.

What drives us

The problem we see

AI agents are being deployed faster than governance frameworks can keep up. Teams are choosing between moving fast and maintaining control. We think that's a false trade-off.

Our approach

We build governance tooling that works at the speed of agent execution — runtime checks, structured approvals, and audit-ready traces that integrate into existing workflows without slowing delivery.
