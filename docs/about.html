About us

We build AI you can put in front of an auditor

Shreeja Technologies is an applied AI delivery company. We design, build, and operate AI agents for organizations that cannot afford a "the model did it" answer.

Mission

Make governed AI the default — so enterprises can ship faster without trading away control.

Approach

Outcome-first delivery: one business workflow, one clear KPI, one governed agent in production within 30 to 45 days.

Strength

AGR — our governance runtime — ships with every engagement. Policy, approvals, and tamper-evident audit are not add-ons.

What we believe

AI adoption fails without operational trust

Most companies do not need another demo. They need reliable execution, controlled behavior, and an audit trail that survives the next regulator visit.

Business before hype

We start from a workflow with a measurable cost or risk, not from a model. Every project has a baseline number and a target number on day one.

Governance by design

Policies, approval gates, and audit chains are scoped in week one and live in production by week four. Not "we'll add it later".

Boring infrastructure

PostgreSQL with row-level security. Hash-chained audit. Durable workflows. We prefer dependable systems over novel ones.

Honest scope

If your use case does not need an agent, we will tell you. If a pilot is the wrong shape, we will redesign it before we quote it.
