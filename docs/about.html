What we believe

Mission, vision, and values

Mission

Enable organizations to deploy AI agents with confidence through clear runtime governance and operational guardrails. Trust should be engineered into systems, not bolted on after the fact.

Vision

A future where trustworthy autonomous systems are standard in enterprise workflows. As AI agents take on more complex tasks, governance must evolve alongside their capabilities.

Values

Trust by design, operational clarity, and responsible human oversight at every stage of adoption. We build for teams who need to ship fast without compromising on accountability.

Context

What drives us

The problem we see

AI agents are being deployed faster than governance frameworks can keep up. Teams are choosing between moving fast and maintaining control. We think that is a false trade-off.

The current state of agentic AI is: powerful models, capable frameworks, and almost no standardized runtime governance. Audit trails are inconsistent. Approval flows are manual. Policy enforcement is ad hoc. This creates real risk as agents get more autonomy.

Our approach

We build governance tooling that works at the speed of agent execution — runtime checks in under 2ms, structured approvals that suspend indefinitely, and audit-ready traces that integrate into existing workflows without slowing delivery.

Our products are developer-first: minimal setup, composable primitives, and no lock-in. AGR can be added to any existing agent framework in a day. Company Box can ship a feature without touching your existing stack.
