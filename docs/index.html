What's inside AGR

Concrete governance, not slideware

Every engagement ships with the same enforcement stack. These are the artifacts your auditor and your CISO actually ask for.

Policy engine

Declarative policies evaluated on every agent action. Allow, deny, or route to approval — versioned, testable, reversible.

Approval workflows

Durable multi-step approvals with reminders, escalation, SLA timers, and email + Slack delivery out of the box.

Hash-chained audit

Every decision recorded with a SHA-256 chain and per-tenant sequence. Tamper-evident and exportable as a signed pack.

Risk scoring

Six-factor risk score on every action with per-org weights and thresholds. Configurable, not hard-coded.

Compliance mapping

Findings mapped to EU AI Act Art. 13, SOC 2 CC6.1, and ISO 42001 §8.4 — advisory or enforcing per organization.

Deployment flexibility

Managed cloud, single-tenant cloud, or fully on-prem with a signed license. Your data stays where your policy requires.
